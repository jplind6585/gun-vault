# Gun Vault App — Roadmap

_Last updated: 2026-03-30_

---

## 🔨 In Progress

- **Maintenance section redesign** — Replacing the maintenance UI with a Log Clean / Log Zero flow using one-tap UX for quick, frictionless logging.
- **Needs Attention strip in Gun Vault** — A status strip in the vault header that flags guns due for cleaning, guns with open issues, and guns unfired for 90+ days.
- **Quick-log session from vault row** — A `+` button on each gun row in the vault opens a session modal for fast range session logging without navigating away.
- **Collection value display** — Shows FMV and insurance value per gun, with totals surfaced in the vault header for a full collection overview.
- **Insurance export** — Generates a claim-ready `.txt` document with the full inventory, values, and relevant details for easy handoff to an insurer.

---

## 🧊 Backburner

- **Gun comparison (side-by-side)** — Spec comparison view for 2–3 guns at once. Planned but deprioritized; will add after higher-priority vault features ship.
- **CSV import modal** — Bulk add guns via file upload or paste with column mapping and a preview step before import. Useful for users migrating from spreadsheets.
- **Purpose tagging per gun** — Tag each gun with its primary use (plinking, EDC, hunting, competition, etc.) and filter the vault by tag.

---

## 💬 Small Mentions

- **Gun comparison side-by-side** — Mentioned in passing; backburnered until after core vault features are stable.
- **AI-generated gun blurbs** — Auto-generate a short description/overview for each gun. Waiting on backend AI integration.
- **Export collection to PDF report** — A formatted PDF export of the full collection for personal records or sharing.
- **Range day summary / session recap view** — A post-session summary screen showing rounds fired, guns used, and notes.
- **Share individual gun loadout card** — Generate a shareable image card with the gun's photo and specs overlay.

---

## 👥 Community Features

_These make sense once the app has a critical mass of users._

- **User profiles and public range logs** — Public-facing profiles with opt-in range session history.
- **Ammo price crowdsourcing** — Community-reported price-per-round by region and retailer to help users find the best deals.
- **Shared gun builds/loadouts with ratings** — Users can publish their builds and the community can rate and comment.
- **Community cleaning schedule benchmarks** — Aggregate data on average rounds before cleaning by make and model, surfaced as a benchmark in the maintenance UI.
- **Ammo lot reviews and reliability ratings** — Per-lot reviews covering feeding, extraction, and consistency, tied to specific ammo lot numbers.
- **Range finder / range locator** — Finds the nearest public ranges by the user's current location.
- **Group buys / cooperative purchase tracking** — Coordinate and track group purchases with other users.
- **Forum or discussion threads per cartridge/platform** — Community threads scoped to specific calibers or gun platforms.

---

## 💡 New Feature Ideas

_Bigger ideas for future consideration._

- **Ballistics calculator** — Real-world conditions calculator with inputs for altitude, temperature, and wind.
- **Reloading tracker** — Track primers, powder, brass, die sets, and load recipes with per-round cost and batch history.
- **FFL dealer locator and transfer tracking** — Find local FFL dealers and log transfer history with cost tracking.
- **Digital 4473 form history log** — Personal log of firearms purchases with dealer, date, and acquisition details for reference.
- **Gun club / range membership tracker** — Track active memberships with renewal date alerts.
- **Maintenance reminder push notifications** — Trigger reminders based on round count thresholds or elapsed time since last cleaning.
- **Insurance auto-valuation via market data API** — Pull sold comps from GunBroker, Rock Island Auction, and similar sources to auto-estimate FMV.
- **Shooting drill library with scoring templates** — Built-in library of standard drills (Bill Drill, Dot Torture, etc.) with scoring input and history.
- **Competition / match tracker** — Log USPSA, IDPA, and PRS matches with stages, scores, and standings over time.
- **Per-gun photo gallery** — Multiple photos per gun with optional date and round-count watermark.
- **Parts tracker per gun** — Track individual components (recoil springs, barrel, trigger parts) with round-count wear estimates and replacement reminders.
- **C&R acquisition log** — Track Curio & Relic acquisitions with cost basis and acquisition documentation for tax and legal record-keeping.
