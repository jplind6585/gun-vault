What's inside

Gun Vault

Catalog every firearm in your collection — make, model, caliber, action, status, photos, and notes. Tracks all-time round count automatically as you log sessions.

Range Session Log

Log every range trip with rounds fired, location, date, and notes. See your activity over the past week, month, or year at a glance.

Ammo Inventory (Arsenal)

Track every ammo lot by caliber, brand, grain weight, bullet type, quantity on hand, and purchase price. Know exactly what you have and what it cost.

Target Analysis

Upload a target photo and plot your shots. The app calculates MOA, extreme spread, CEP, and standard deviation — the same metrics competitive shooters use to measure precision.

Armory Assistant

An AI assistant scoped to your vault data and general firearms questions. Ask about your gear, get load recommendations, or look up cartridge specs.

Field Guide

Reference library covering calibers, optics, competition formats (USPSA, IDPA, PRS), marksmanship fundamentals, and ballistics. Built in, no internet required.

Free. No subscription. Always improving.
